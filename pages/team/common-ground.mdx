## Core Hours

Our working hours are fixed from 8:30 to 18:00, Monday to Friday, with an 8-hour workday excluding the lunch break (12:00 to 13:30). Everyone is required to start working at the office by 8:30 at the latest.

As we operate within the service industry, it is imperative for us to ensure our availability to clients during their business hours. Therefore, each project team should be able to request (to the accountant and director) the modification of their working time frame to align with client requirements, facilitating demonstrations, discussions, and deployments.

> Those core hours are 8:30 to 18:00 (Hanoi/Vietnam time, GMT+7).

The goal is to have all teammates together and available for each other for at least 8 hours/day.

## Food
